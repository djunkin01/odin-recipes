Slow-Cooker Squirrel

Roadkill for when you just get that hankerin'...

This recipe was developed with a team of sensory scientists at MIT under direct supervision from the CEO of Taco Bell.

As referenced from the picture, it is a "eat with your eyes first" type of dish, sure to impress that date you decided to cook for

Ingredients
  • 1 ea squirrel
  • 1 qt water
  • 1 ea carrot
  • 1 ea onion
  • 12 ea tears of remorse
Steps
  1. Remove fur from squirrel
  2. Boil everything together until fragrant
  3. Pray
  4. Enjoy!
