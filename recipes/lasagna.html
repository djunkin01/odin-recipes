Lasagna

Three cheese lasagna with fresh herbs

This recipe has been passed down through generations of my family. Over the years it was lost, found, lost again, until finally I said "To Hell with it" and created my own. So this recipe is actually pretty new

Starting with a bang and ending with a smush, this recipe is likely to inspire religious inspiration

Ingredients
  • 12 ea pasta sheets
  • 1 qt tomato sauce
  • 2 oz basil
  • 1 pound "meat"
  • Enough cheese to kill a man
Steps
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil the pasta sheets
  3. Layer the sheets with the tomato sauce and "meat"
  4. Top with cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 24-46 hours
  5. Garnish with basil and enjoy!
