African Peanut Stew

A stew to stir the soul and awaken the libido

This recipe has also been passed down through generations of my family. Over the years it was lost, found, lost again, until finally I said "To Hell with it" and created my own. So this recipe is actually pretty new

Starting with a bang and ending with a smush, this recipe is likely to inspire religious inspiration

Ingredients
  • 1 gallon broth
  • 1 handful peanuts
  • 1 ea cow
  • 2 oz exotic spices
  • Salt. Lots of salt.
Steps
  1. Kill and butcher the cow, fabricating into the different sub-primal cuts
  2. Boil the stew meat in the broth for 3-4 days, depending on altitude
  3. Add spices and boil another 3-4 days
  4. Add salt to taste and garnish with peanuts
  5. Enjoy!
